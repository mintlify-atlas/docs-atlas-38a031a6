Welcome to your NutriFit patient portal. This guide will help you make the most of your account and manage your nutrition journey.

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, your dashboard displays:

- **Statistics**:
  - Total appointments you've had
  - Completed consultations
  - Pending appointments

- **Next Appointment**: Details of your upcoming consultation including:
  - Nutritionist name
  - Date and time
  - Appointment type
  - Quick access to appointment details

- **Recent Appointments**: Your 10 most recent consultations

- **Available Nutritionists**: Browse nutritionists accepting appointments

<Note>
You can only have one pending appointment at a time. Complete or cancel your current appointment before booking another.
</Note>

## First Time Setup

### Changing Your Password

When you first log in with a default password:

1. You'll be prompted to change your password
2. Enter a new password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Confirm your new password
4. Click **Update Password**

You'll receive an email confirmation of the password change.

### Completing Your Profile

1. Click **Profile** in the navigation
2. Update your information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Date of birth
   - Gender
   - Emergency contact
   - Medical history (optional but recommended)
3. Upload profile photo (optional)
4. Click **Save Profile**

<Tip>
Providing complete medical history helps your nutritionist create better personalized plans.
</Tip>

## Booking Appointments

### Finding a Nutritionist

1. Click **Book Appointment** from dashboard or navigation
2. Browse available nutritionists
3. View each nutritionist's:
   - Name and credentials
   - Specializations
   - Consultation price
   - Availability status
4. Click **View Schedule** to see their available times

### Selecting Appointment Time

1. After selecting a nutritionist, you'll see their calendar
2. The calendar shows 4 weeks of availability
3. Available time slots are displayed by:
   - Week number (current week and next 3 weeks)
   - Day of the week
   - Specific times (45-minute slots)
4. Click on an available time slot
5. Unavailable or booked slots are grayed out

### Completing Your Booking

1. Confirm your selected date and time
2. Choose appointment type:
   - **First Visit**: Your initial consultation
   - **Follow-up**: Regular follow-up session  
   - **Control**: Check-up or monitoring visit
3. Enter reason for visit (optional but helpful)
4. Review appointment details:
   - Nutritionist name
   - Date and time
   - Price
5. Click **Confirm Appointment**

You'll receive:
- Immediate confirmation on screen
- Email confirmation with appointment details
- Reminder notifications before your appointment

<Warning>
You cannot book a new appointment while you have a pending appointment. Complete or cancel your existing appointment first.
</Warning>

### Appointment Types Explained

**First Visit (Primera Vez)**:
- Comprehensive initial assessment
- Complete medical and dietary history
- Initial measurements and goals setting
- Personalized nutrition plan creation
- Usually longer and more detailed

**Follow-up (Seguimiento)**:
- Regular check-in on progress
- Adjustments to nutrition plan
- New measurements and progress tracking
- Discussion of challenges and successes

**Control (Control)**:
- Quick check-up session
- Verify adherence to plan
- Answer questions
- Minor adjustments if needed

## Managing Your Appointments

### Viewing Appointment Details

1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. Click on any appointment to view:
   - Nutritionist information
   - Date and time
   - Status (pending, completed, cancelled, expired)
   - Appointment type and reason
   - Price
   - Location or meeting link
   - Clinical notes (for completed appointments)

### Filtering Appointments

Use filters to find specific appointments:

- **By Status**: Pending, completed, cancelled, expired
- **By Nutritionist**: See appointments with specific nutritionist
- **By Date Range**: Select start and end dates

### Cancelling an Appointment

If you need to cancel:

1. Open the appointment details
2. Click **Cancel Appointment** button
3. Confirm the cancellation

Notifications are sent:
- You receive immediate confirmation
- Your nutritionist is notified
- The time slot becomes available for other patients

<Warning>
Cancelled appointments cannot be recovered. You'll need to book a new appointment.
</Warning>

### Appointment States Explained

- **Pending**: Scheduled and confirmed, awaiting the appointment date
- **Completed**: Consultation finished, clinical records available
- **Cancelled**: Appointment was cancelled by you or your nutritionist
- **Expired**: Appointment time passed without completion (automatically marked)

## After Your Consultation

### Accessing Your Clinical Records

1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. Find the completed appointment
3. Click to view details
4. Clinical data includes:
   - Body measurements (weight, height, BMI)
   - Circumference measurements
   - Body composition analysis
   - Metabolic calculations
   - Clinical notes from nutritionist
   - Personalized nutrition plan
   - Follow-up recommendations

### Downloading Reports

For completed consultations:

1. Open the appointment
2. Click **Download PDF** or **View PDF**
3. PDF includes:
   - All measurements and data
   - Nutritionist's notes and recommendations
   - Your personalized nutrition plan
   - Next steps and goals

Save these reports to track your progress over time.

## Viewing Your Health History

### Accessing Your History

1. Click **My History** in navigation
2. View all your completed consultations
3. See interactive progress charts

### Understanding Progress Charts

Your history includes visual tracking of:

**Weight and Body Composition**:
- Weight progression over time
- BMI changes
- Body fat percentage trends

**Measurements**:
- Waist circumference
- Hip circumference
- Arm measurements (contracted and relaxed)
- Leg measurements (thigh and calf)
- Neck and wrist measurements

**Metabolic Metrics**:
- TMB (Basal Metabolic Rate) changes
- TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure)
- Target calorie adjustments

**Body Ratios**:
- WHR (Waist-to-Hip Ratio)
- WHT (Waist-to-Height Ratio)

### Progress Statistics

View your overall progress:

- **Total Consultations**: Number of sessions completed
- **First Visit Date**: When you started
- **Last Visit Date**: Your most recent consultation
- **Weight Change**: Total weight lost/gained and percentage
- **BMI Change**: How your BMI has improved
- **Body Fat Change**: Reduction in body fat percentage
- **Measurement Changes**: Centimeter changes in key areas

<Tip>
Review your progress charts before appointments to discuss specific concerns with your nutritionist.
</Tip>

1. Navigate to **Profile**

   - Email address

   - Gender

### Managing Notification Preferences

Control which notifications you receive:

- Appointment reminders (24 hours before)
- Cancellation notifications
- Reschedule notifications
- General updates from your nutritionist

You'll automatically receive emails for:

### Appointment-Related
- **Booking Confirmation**: When you book an appointment
- **Reminder**: 24 hours before appointment
- **Cancellation**: When appointment is cancelled
- **Reschedule**: When appointment time changes
- **Completion**: When consultation is finished and records are available

### Account-Related
- **Password Changed**: Security notification
- **Account Status**: If account is activated/deactivated
- **Profile Updates**: Confirmation of profile changes

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Book Appointment

**Issue**: "You already have a pending appointment" message

**Solution**: 
- Check your dashboard for existing appointments
- Complete or cancel your current appointment
- Only one pending appointment allowed at a time

### Nutritionist Has No Available Times

**Solution**:
- Check if they have configured their schedule
- Try a different week (calendar shows 4 weeks)
- Consider booking with another nutritionist
- Contact the clinic for assistance

### Cannot Access Clinical Records

**Issue**: Records not showing for completed appointment

**Solution**:
- Ensure appointment status is "completed"
- Refresh the page
- Wait a few minutes (records may be processing)
- Contact your nutritionist if issue persists

### Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click link and create new password
5. Log in with new password

## Best Practices

### Before Your Appointment

- Review your progress history
- Prepare questions for your nutritionist
- Note any challenges or concerns
- Bring previous reports if you have them
- Arrive on time (or log in on time for virtual appointments)

### During Your Appointment

- Be honest about your eating habits
- Ask questions if you don't understand
- Take notes on recommendations
- Discuss realistic goals
- Request clarification on the nutrition plan

### After Your Appointment

- Download and save your PDF report
- Review the nutrition plan thoroughly
- Set reminders for follow-up tasks
- Track your daily progress
- Book your next follow-up appointment

### Ongoing Management

- Check dashboard regularly
- Keep profile information current
- Monitor your progress charts
- Attend all scheduled appointments
- Communicate with your nutritionist if issues arise

## Quick Actions

- **Book Appointment**: Dashboard → Book Appointment → Select Nutritionist
- **View Next Appointment**: Check dashboard for quick details
- **Cancel Appointment**: My Appointments → Select Appointment → Cancel
- **Download Report**: My Appointments → Completed Appointment → Download PDF
- **View Progress**: My History → View Charts and Statistics
- **Update Profile**: Profile → Edit → Save Changes
- **Change Password**: Profile → Change Password

<Tip>
Set calendar reminders for your appointments and check your email regularly for important notifications.
</Tip>

## Privacy and Security

Your health information is protected:

- All data is encrypted
- Only you and your nutritionist can access your records
- Administrators can view appointment information for support purposes
- Your data is never shared without consent
- You can request data export or deletion at any time

## Getting Help

If you need assistance:

- Contact your nutritionist directly for clinical questions
- Use the contact form for technical support
- Check FAQs for common questions
- Email support for account issues

Your health journey is important to us. Don't hesitate to reach out if you need help navigating the system.
